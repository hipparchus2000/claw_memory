## How the System Works:

### 1. **Listening Phase** (Continuous):
- Monitors Slack conversations
- Extracts topics and keywords
- Tracks engagement (reactions, replies)

### 2. **Research Phase** (5:00am UTC):
- Researches detected topics
- Finds relevant information
- Prepares natural drop-ins

### 3. **Integration Phase** (Conversational):
- Waits for relevant context
- Drops insights naturally
- Uses conversational language
